What is GST ?

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax.It is a comprehensive indirect tax system that has been implemented by several governments worldwide.The primary objective of GST is to streamline the taxation structure and replace multiple indirect taxes levied by the government.
The GST system operates through three main comnponents:
1. Central GST(CGST):This component of GST is levied by the central government on intra-state supplies of goods and services.The revenue collected under CGST goes to the central government.
2. State GST(SGST):SGST is levied by the state government on intra-state supplies of goods and services.The revenue collected under SGST goes to the respective state government.
3. Integrated GST(IGST):IGST is applicable to inter-state supplies of goods and services, as well as imports.It is collected by the central government and distributed between the central and state governments based on the predetermined revenue-sharing mechanisms.
